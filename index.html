About Me

Zhion Chisenga

"Zion Chisenga, a native of Zambia, is a writer and biomedical engineer. His passion for writing began during his college years, leading him to create articles, essays, and poems for various contexts. He's also recognized as a certified peer educator under the Ministry of Health. Chisenga's journey highlights the synergy between his literary pursuits and scientific background. His narrative emphasizes the power of self-cultivated value in shaping our destinies, much like bending the course of nature. From his Zambian roots to his academic journey, he embodies the fusion of creativity and technical acumen. Through his written expressions, he conveys that nurturing individual worth resonates with the universe, driving success and fulfillment."
